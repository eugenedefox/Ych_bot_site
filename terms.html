Terms for using YCH Commishes Bot.

These terms explain the basic rules for using YCH Commishes Bot, a Telegram assistant for artwork publishing, auction links, bid tracking, and connected social platforms.

Last updated: May 26, 2026

1. Service description

YCH Commishes Bot helps users prepare and publish artwork posts from Telegram to connected platforms chosen by the user. The bot may help with titles, descriptions, tags, auction links, templates, bid tracking, and publishing previews.

The bot is an independent tool. It is not an official product of Instagram, Meta, Tumblr, DeviantArt, Bluesky, Discord, Telegram, YCH Commishes, or any other connected platform.

2. User responsibility

Users are responsible for the content they upload, prepare, and publish through the bot. By using the bot, users agree that they have the rights and permissions needed to publish the artwork, text, tags, links, and other materials they provide.

  • Do not use the bot to publish stolen artwork or copyrighted material without permission.
  • Do not use the bot for spam, scams, harassment, or illegal activity.
  • Do not use the bot to violate the rules of any connected platform.
  • Check the preview before publishing, because the user controls the final post.

3. Connected accounts and publishing

The bot publishes to connected platforms only after the user connects an account or enables a configured destination, prepares a post, and confirms publishing in Telegram. The bot does not sell user data and does not use connected platform access for advertising or profiling.

Platform availability may depend on third-party APIs, platform permissions, review status, rate limits, outages, or token expiration. Publishing may fail if a connected platform rejects the request or changes its API behavior.

4. Account disconnection and data

Users may disconnect supported integrations through bot commands when available. For Instagram, users can send /disconnect_instagram to remove the stored Instagram access token from the bot.

Privacy and data deletion details are available in the Privacy Policy and Data Deletion sections.

5. Changes and availability

The bot may be changed, paused, limited, or discontinued at any time. Features may be added or removed as platform APIs, security requirements, or user needs change.

The bot is provided as a practical workflow helper. No guarantee is made that every post, auction link, notification, or integration will always work without error.
